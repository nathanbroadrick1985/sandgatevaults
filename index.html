Salutations!!

A little about us... The building that now houses the ‘Sandgate Vaults Restaurant’, was reportedly built in 1860. It has had many owners, from a Pharmacists and Savings Bank to an Oriental take-away to name but a few. We have tried to keep it as original as possible. In the Vault Bar, there is a wall that is layered in slate and it was thought that the shelves there were for the purpose of keeping the medication prepared by the pharmacist cool. In keeping the buildings character, we have created a quirky & charismatic ‘Vault Bar’.
